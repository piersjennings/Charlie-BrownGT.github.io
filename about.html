About Me

Hello, my name is Guy Carter-Richardson. I was silly enough to share that I had a website to the RT, and now I have ended up with a bunch of RT opperators messing up with my website.

I completed my undergraduate and masters studies in Physics at Swansea University, where I actively participated in various research projects and gained valuable insights into experimental and theoretical aspects of physics. These experiences only intensified my passion for the subject, and I am now eager to take my knowledge and skills to the next level by pursuing a Ph.D. in Physics.

Why I Want to Pursue a Ph.D. in Physics

I am particularly interested in exploring topics such as quantum mechanics, cosmology, and high-energy particle physics.

I am excited about the potential to conduct new research, collaborate with researchers, and be part of a vibrant academic community. The opportunity to unravel the complexities of the universe through rigorous scientific inquiry is an exciting opportunity that I hope to begin in the coming 12 months.

Moreover, I am eager to engage in teaching and mentorship, as I believe that sharing knowledge and inspiring the next generation of physicists is a crucial aspect of academic growth and progress.
